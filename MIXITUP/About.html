WHAT IT DOES

Do you have leftover drink from that last party you had? Want to have fun mixing drinks at home? Time to Mix it up! Using the ingredients you already have, we can show you how to create the best cocktails, whilst helping you to get rid of your leftover drink.

WHO WE ARE

We're five slightly alcoholic students developing a solution to a much suffered problem experienced the world over. Using our combined skills in web design, coding and good looks we have created Mix It Up to help better humanity.

HOW TO USE

Finding the cocktails for you couldn't be simpler. Just select all the ingredients you have from the list and hit MIX. We will show you all the best and nicest cocktails that you can make without even having to leave the house!
